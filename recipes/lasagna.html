Lasagna

Description

Lasagna so good your ex will want you back. Unlike them, this recipe is simple and won't leave you for your best friend.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mix.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Bake.
